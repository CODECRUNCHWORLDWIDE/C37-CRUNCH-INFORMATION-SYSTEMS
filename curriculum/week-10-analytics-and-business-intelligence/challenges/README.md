# Week 10 — Challenges

Two challenges. Both stretch past the exercises' guardrails into judgment-call territory — real analytics-engineering work, where the hard part is rarely writing the `INSERT` and almost always deciding what's actually true.

## How to work these

- Challenge 1 is a **code + judgment** exercise — a messy, realistic source dataset you must clean, dedupe, and load without silently losing or double-counting anyone.
- Challenge 2 is a **critique + code** exercise — a working dashboard that computes a KPI correctly, in the narrow technical sense, while telling a misleading story. You find the mismatch and fix it.
- Neither has one right answer graded against a key. Both are graded on whether your reasoning is sound, your assumptions are stated, and your fix actually holds up against the data.
