# Week 9 — Challenges

Two challenges. Both stretch past the exercises' guardrails into judgment-call territory — real security and privacy work, where the hard part is rarely "do I know the technique" and almost always "which of ten real risks actually deserve my limited time this week."

## How to work these

- Challenge 1 is an **analysis** exercise — no required code, a structured threat-modeling document with a ranked risk table.
- Challenge 2 is a **redesign** exercise — you take a deliberately non-compliant system description and produce both a corrected schema/policy and a written explanation of every fix.
- Neither has one right answer. Both are graded on whether your reasoning is sound, your assumptions are stated, and your priorities are defensible — not on matching a key.
