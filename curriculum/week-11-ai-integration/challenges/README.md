# Week 11 Challenges

Two challenges. Both assume Exercises 1–3 are done — the risk model is trained and saved, and `ask_crunchcycles` works end to end.

| # | Challenge | Stretches |
|---|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | [challenge-01-human-in-the-loop-design.md](./challenge-01-human-in-the-loop-design.md) | Turns Lecture 3's HITL levels into a real reviewed-workflow implementation for a genuinely risky decision |
| 2 | [challenge-02-evaluate-an-ai-feature.md](./challenge-02-evaluate-an-ai-feature.md) | Builds a real, runnable evaluation harness for both AI features — the numbers a stakeholder would actually ask to see |

## How to work these

These are harder and less scaffolded than the exercises — you'll make design decisions the exercises handed you, and there isn't one single correct answer for either challenge. Write down your reasoning, not just your code; the reasoning is most of what's being assessed. Both challenges plug directly into the mini-project, so budget real time for them rather than rushing to Saturday.
