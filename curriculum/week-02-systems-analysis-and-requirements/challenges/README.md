# Week 2 Challenges

Two open-ended problems. Unlike the exercises, there is no single correct answer — a senior analyst and a junior analyst will produce different (both defensible) resolutions. What's judged is the quality of your reasoning and how precisely you write down your assumptions, not whether you match a hidden key.

## How to work these

- Read the whole scenario before writing anything — both challenges have a trap that's easy to miss on a fast read.
- Every judgment call you make must be written down as an explicit assumption. An answer with no stated assumptions is an incomplete answer, even if the SQL runs and the prose reads well.
- Use everything from this week's lectures — laddering, MoSCoW, the requirement-quality checklist, use cases, INVEST — these are designed to need more than one skill at once.
